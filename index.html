Services

Let me tell you about the services i render

Line Editing

This entails looking over the manuscript sentence by sentence, making comments on word choice, syntax, and tone to improve the author's writing style and the overall impact of the text.

Copyediting

Copyediting deals with correcting mistakes relating to punctuation, tone, style discrepancies, spelling and grammar mistakes.

Proofreading

This deals with the reviewing of the final draft of a book before it goes to the press, basically to catch every error that the copyeditor missed as regards  consistency and accuracy in grammar, spelling, punctuation, and layout.

Academic Proofreading

This entails the final editing and formatting of a publication manuscript to ensure little mistakes that can result in desk rejection are avoided, and consistency in reference style is ensured.
